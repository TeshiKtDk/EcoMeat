What are we?

One of our project ideas is a meat imitation. It will be made with only vegetable-origin ingredients,  which will make this less expensive than actual meat, and it will have the nutrients a piece of meat  provides. To make the base of the meat we will be making seitan, a nutritious dough that will provide us with some good proteins! And we will be using more ingredients as well because the more ingredients added to the mix the more nutrients and proteins we get! Moderating everything of course so the meal is healthy!

Why are we made of?

Setain which is food made from gluten also known as the main protein that wheat carries. One of the reasons why we are using these ingredients is because they are accessible to everyone, as it can be really easy to find them and they are cheap in most places! Wheat is used for obtaining the gluten it has and using it to make our nutritious meat base. So therefore water is a method to get the dough hydrated and allow it to be handled as we please and allowing us to proceed with the method needed to make the base for the meat.

Our Goals

Our main goal is to make certain foods more accesible and for them to have the same nutritional value as others
